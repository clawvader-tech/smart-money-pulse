Hi Hugging Face Team,

Big fan of what you've built—HF has become the GitHub of AI. I'm reaching out because **Nexus AI** is building something we think complements your mission perfectly: a community-owned network for distributed AI training and inference.

### The Opportunity
We noticed HF doesn't currently offer:
- Distributed training across consumer GPUs
- Token-based incentives for model contributors  
- Decentralized inference at scale

That's exactly what Nexus AI provides, and we'd love to integrate deeply with your ecosystem.

### Proposed Integration

**1. Model Hub Sync**
- Nexus-trained models auto-published to HF Model Hub
- Attribution to contributing GPU providers in model cards
- Dual licensing: Open source + commercial for contributors

**2. Dataset Marketplace**
- HF datasets integrated into Nexus training pipeline
- Dataset creators earn $NEXUS when their data improves models
- Revenue share: 60% to dataset authors, 40% to Nexus

**3. Inference API Bridge**
- Nexus GPU network as backend for HF Inference API
- Lower costs than centralized providers
- Revenue share on inference calls

**4. Community Rewards**
- HF users earn $NEXUS for:
  - Uploading quality datasets
  - Contributing model fine-tunes
  - Providing compute via Spaces GPU

### Technical Details
Our stack:
- Base: Qwen2.5-2B (consumer-hardware friendly)
- Training: PEFT + LoRA for distributed fine-tuning
- Inference: vLLM with dynamic batching
- Blockchain: Base L2 with Superfluid streaming rewards

### Pilot Program Proposal
Let's start small:
1. **Month 1:** Technical integration POC - sync 5 models to HF Hub
2. **Month 2:** Dataset marketplace beta with 10 curated datasets
3. **Month 3:** Full integration launch with community announcement
